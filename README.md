项目概览 | Project Overview
 
本项目融合前沿人工智能技术与超声影像诊断技术，打造专业的上消化道无创AI辅助诊断方案，依托口服超声造影剂与充盈超声造影检查方法，实现胃部、胰腺等器官病变精准筛查与辅助诊断，全面提升临床诊疗效率，改善患者就医体验，助力基层医疗智能化发展。
 
Integrating cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology with ultrasound imaging diagnosis technology, this project develops a professional non-invasive AI-assisted diagnosis solution for the upper gastrointestinal tract. Relying on oral ultrasound contrast agents and filling ultrasound contrast examination methods, it achieves accurate screening and auxiliary diagnosis of lesions in organs such as the stomach and pancreas, comprehensively improves clinical diagnosis and treatment efficiency, enhances patient medical experience, and empowers the intelligent development of primary medical care.

系统架构 | System Architecture
 
项目采用前后端分离的模块化架构设计，分为前端交互展示模块、后端业务服务模块、AI智能推理模块、数据管理模块四大核心板块，保障系统运行稳定、易于维护，同时具备极强的功能拓展与场景适配能力。
 
The project adopts a modular architecture design with separated front-end and back-end, divided into four core sections: front-end interactive display module, back-end business service module, AI intelligent reasoning module, and data management module. It ensures stable system operation and easy maintenance, while having strong function expansion and scenario adaptation capabilities.
