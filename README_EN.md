# Aimed AGI Ultrasound Documentation (English)  

## Project Overview  
FillingVision (AIMED Agent Swarm for upper GI tract AI diagnosis) is a pioneering initiative that leverages artificial intelligence to enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency for gastrointestinal conditions, specifically targeting pancreatic and gastric diseases.

## Core Features  
- **Pancreatic and Gastric Diagnosis**: Specialized AI models developed for effective diagnosis of pancreatic and gastric conditions.  
- **Multi-Agent Collaborative System**: A system that utilizes multiple AI agents working collaboratively to improve diagnostic outcomes.

## Technical Advantages  
- **AI-Physician Consistency Rate**: Achieves an impressive consistency rate of 85-95% between AI and physician diagnoses.  
- **Clinical Efficiency Improvement**: Over 50% improvement in clinical efficiency, allowing healthcare providers to serve more patients in less time.  
- **Cost Reduction**: Reduces operational costs by approximately 80%, making advanced diagnostics more accessible.  
- **Inference Speed**: Delivers results in just 1 second, ensuring timely decision-making in critical situations.

## Application Features  
- **Universal Hardware Compatibility**: Seamlessly integrates with various medical devices and systems.  
- **Lightweight Inference Engine**: Optimized for performance without compromising on accuracy, suitable for deployment in various settings.  
- **Standardized Workflow**: Facilitates a streamlined and efficient workflow for healthcare professionals.
